ABOUT

What is BUILD Society?

BUILD (Building Urban Intelligence with Learning Data) is a platform for urban planners, technologists, and innovators aimed at harnessing data and technology to tackle urban challenges. We foster a culture of collaboration to create efficient, sustainable, and livable urban spaces.

What is the Community all about?

BUILD Society's community is at the heart of our mission to foster urban intelligence. It's a diverse group of individuals who are passionate about leveraging data, sharing knowledge, and working together to solve the urban challenges of tomorrow.
